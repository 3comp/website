## What is Odoo ERP?

Odoo ERP is a comprehensive business software platform designed to connect and manage all major operational areas within a company. ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems unify business data, workflows, and communication into one integrated platform, eliminating the need for multiple disconnected tools.

Odoo offers a wide range of business applications, including:

- Accounting and financial management
- Customer relationship management (CRM)
- Sales and purchasing management
- Inventory and warehouse management
- Manufacturing and production planning
- Project and service management
- Human resources and payroll
- Marketing automation and eCommerce
- Business analytics and reporting

Thanks to its modular structure, Odoo allows companies to start with core business applications and expand functionality as their business grows. Odoo can be deployed as a cloud-based solution or installed on local infrastructure, providing flexibility for different operational and security requirements.
