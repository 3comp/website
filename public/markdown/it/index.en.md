## What is IT Support?

IT support involves maintaining, troubleshooting, and optimizing computer systems, hardware, software, and digital environments to ensure reliable performance and security. Regular IT maintenance helps prevent system failures, improves performance, and extends the lifespan of devices.

Professional IT support helps users:

- Improve computer speed and performance
- Fix software and hardware issues
- Maintain secure and stable operating systems
- Organize and protect important data
- Receive guidance when purchasing or upgrading devices

IT support services ensure that technology remains reliable, efficient, and easy to use.
